About Us

Dear readers,

TheBookHouse is one of the newest bookstores in Cape Town, it was established few years ago back in 2020 during COVID-19 pandemic. The online bookstore was founded by two scholars who live in the township of Old Cross Roads, Nyanga.

We offer wide range of educational books which help each and every individual with personal, professional development and we also offer books of money management, wealth accumulation.
